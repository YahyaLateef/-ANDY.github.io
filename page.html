HOW TO BUY

MAKE A WALLET

Get started by downloading Phantom or your preferred wallet from the app store or Google Play for free. For desktop users, install the Phantom extension from the Chrome Web Store.

BUY SOME SOL

Ensure you have SOL in your wallet to exchange for $SAMI. If you don't have any, you can purchase SOL directly within the Phantom app, transfer from another wallet, or buy it on a different exchange and send it to your wallet.

GO TO RAYDIUM

Access Raydium. Open Raydium in your Chrome browser or within the Phantom app browser. Connect your wallet. Enter the $SAMI token address in Raydium, select $SAMI, and confirm. When Phantom asks for a wallet signature, approve it.

SWAP SOL FOR $ANDY

Trade SOL for $ANDY. With zero taxes, you don't need to worry about setting a specific slippage for the transaction.
